Event Overview

SMCC-CCIS Week 2026 is an annual campus event that highlights student projects, programming skills, innovation, cybersecurity
awareness, and collaboration, among future IT professionals.

Join us for a week of learning, networking, and fun & excitement as we celebrate technology in education

Event Schedule

Here is the simplified schedule of activities:

  • Day 1 : Opening Program and Keynote Speech
  • Day 2 : Coding hackathon
  • Day 3 : Robot and iot exhibition
  • Day 4 : Cybersecurity Awareness Session
  • Day 5 : Project Showcase and Awarding Ceremony
